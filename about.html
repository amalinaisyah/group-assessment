ABOUT US

The Modern Mode Company was founded in Malaysia by Wan Nur Athirah Binti Wan Mohd Rosly in 2020. Athirah noticed that majority of the apparel company in Malaysia does not offer trendy, comfortable and affordable apparel for woman and man. She decided to make her brand and ask her friends to join her. Modern Mode means for now or in the other words, it can be said that all of the apparel offered by Modern Mode are trendy. Other than that, Modern Mode is an online based company so they do not have any physical store that people can drop by.
